Gingka Hagane

Gingka Hagane is a Beyblader from Japan. He and his Beyblade, Pegasus, take on challenges from other Beybladers. His goal is to create friendships through Beyblading and defeat organizations like Dark Nebula who try to exploit Beybladers for their personal gain. Throughout the show he has a positive and courageous attitude and always maintains the blader's spirit. Biography of Gingka by Beyblade Fandom.

  • He is a courageous blader who never gives up.
  • He does his best to make friends while achieving his goal.
  • He always tries to help others and learn from other bladers.
